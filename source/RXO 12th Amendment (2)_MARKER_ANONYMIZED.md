## a. **2023 / 2024 Peak Capacity Dedicated Trucks**

- a. Peak Season will operate 11/13/23 through 12/23/2023, Samsung and RXO agree to increase the original truck count by the requested total truck counts in the table below.
- b. Samsung and RXO will review truck productivity the week of 12/11/23 to review Dedicated Truck counts beyond 12/23/23.
- c. Stops per Truck
  - i. Appliance and TV Markets: 12 stops per day/truck to include Next Day operations.
  - ii. Appliance Markets: 10 Stops per day/truck to include Next Day operations.
  - iii. All Markets will operate 6 days per week excluding Holidays.
- d. Additional trucks will be added and invoiced daily based on the Rules of engagement below.
  - i. When volume exceeds Dedicated Truck Capacity by ≥ 5 stops, an additional Dedicated Truck will be used and billed (when available)
  - ii. If additional Dedicated truck is not available, the shared network will be used, (shared network is defined and this current Last Mile Hub co-mingled network that is delivering Samsung deliveries)
  - iii. When volume exceeds Dedicated Truck Capacity by ≤ 4 stops, the excess volume will be diverted to Shared Network
  - iv. When volume exceeds Dedicated Capacity by ≥ 5 stops for 4 consecutive weeks, an additional truck will be added into the Dedicated Network
  - v. Standard Fuel Surcharge, Installation Parts and/or any additional Accessorial charges are not contained in the below rates.
