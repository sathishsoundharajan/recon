## **b. Returns Product Wrapping and Testing:**

- **a.** The below rates under this Section 2(b) of this Agreement have been agreed to by both Samsung and RXOLM under this Amendment.
- **b.** RXOLM shall prepare the customer appliance return units with corner post/shrink-wrap, with or without bubble wrap and fill out the "Unit Return Form" for each unit.
  - **i.** Rate: All Appliance types: \$22.83 per unit with bubble wrap until December 1, 2024.
  - **ii.** Rate: All Appliance types: \$16.83 per unit without bubble wrap beginning November 3, 2024.
- **c.** RXOLM shall prepare customer TV return units with corner protectors, bubble wrap, fill out Unit Return Form(s), and shall reuse or provide new TV returns box for each unit.
  - **i.** Rate:
    - **a)** RXOLM provides new TV box: \$39.56 per unit.
    - **b)** RXOLM uses existing TV box: \$15.66 per unit.
    - **c)** RXOLM to plug-in TV and confirm power-up: \$3.52 per unit.
- b. Samsung associate rewraps appliance or TV unit utilizing RXOLM materials: \$9.55 per unit.

## **c. Field Destroy (Scrap) of Return Units:**

- d. When requested by Samsung, RXOLM shall field destroy appliance and TV return units following the below process:
  - i. Remove/deface Samsung logo.
  - ii. Remove serial number and rating labels.
    - a) The serial number and rating labels are to be placed on paper and stored for seven (7) years.
  - iii. Take photos of progress and completion.
  - iv. Photos and details to be uploaded into Samsungs N-Pass App.
  - v. Mark "X" on front, one side and inside product via spray paint.
  - vi. Provide Certificate of Destruction upon request from recycler.
- e. Per unit scrap costs:
  - i. Appliance per unit: \$47.78 (includes recycling)
  - ii. TV per unit: \$42.78 + recycling fee below.
    - a) Regular sized TVs (TVs less than or equal to 65" diagonal) \$102.35 per TV.
    - b) Oversized TVs (TVs greater than 65" diagonal) \$119.60 per TV.
    - c) Pricing for other TVs quote basis:
      - a. TVs that are over 200 lbs.
      - b. Older/bulkier screen types (CRT, projection, etc.).
    - d) Palletization of scrap TVs shipped back to SAMSUNG location: \$14.25 per TV.
      - a. This rate only covers the palletization of the scrap TVs.

## **f. On-Time Delivery, Penalty and Incentive**

- Both RXOLM and Samsung have mutually agreed to a Penalty and Incentive scheme based on On-Time Delivery Performance.
- Data source shall be sourced from RXOLM Performance Scorecard (CLM), utilizing the Adjusted On-Time to Original Delivery Date.
- Will reconcile results and pay/receive the incentive/penalty monthly. Utilizing the working week numbers.
- RXOLM shall provide Samsung a final OTD to ORDD report by the fifth (5 th) day of every month.
  - i. Reports shall include order level detail, exception codes and details on why delay happened and how the owner of the delay was determined (Samsung, RXOLM or customer).
- Samsung shall then have seven (7) days to review the report and work through any corrections with RXOLM.
- Result shall be finalized by the fifteenth (15th) of each month and reconciliation should occur before the thirtieth (30th) of the month.
- RXOLM and Samsung shall revisit the structure of the program on a quarterly basis and make adjustments which shall mutually agreed upon in writing with written approval from Samsung and RXOLM VP levels or higher.
- RXOLM and Samsung shall agree to change the data source for the program to Samsung's internal data thirty (30) days after both Parties internal delivery and installation tools are fully operational.
- The structure shall be as follows:
  - i. Hubs are categorized into 3 tiers, based on market share volume: large, medium and small:
    - 1. Large: ≥ 2.5%
    - 2. Medium: 2.4% to 1.0%
    - 3. Small: ≤ 1.0%
  - ii. Achievement Criteria is set as follows:
    - 1. ≥ 92% = incentive
    - 2. 84.0% 91.9% = no incentive, no penalty
    - 3. < 83.9% = penalty
  - iii. Incentive amounts are as follows:
    - 1. Large tier = \$1,700 / mo.
    - 2. Medium tier = \$900 / mo.
    - 3. Small tier = \$300 / mo.
  - iv. Penalty amounts are as follows:
    - 1. Large, medium, small = \$625 / mo.
  - v. RXOLM may also qualify fora special achievement incentive for achieving an on-time delivery rate of ≥ 95% (market must have a minimum of seventy-five (75) orders during the period to be eligible for this Incentive):
    - 1. Large tier = \$1000 / Mo.
    - 2. Medium tier = \$500 / Mo.
    - 3. Small tier = \$200 / Mo.
  - vi. Annual maximum incentive/penalty caps will be in place and will be as follows:
    - 1. Incentive (Samsung → RXOLM): \$502,800 / annual
    - 2. Penalty (RXOLM → Samsung): \$502,800 / annual
    - 3. Special achievement incentive (Samsung → RXOLM): \$200,000
