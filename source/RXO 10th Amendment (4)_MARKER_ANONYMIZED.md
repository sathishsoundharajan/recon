## 2023 Dedicated Truck Discount.

Both Parties agree to the following Dedicated Truck rate discount, defined as the following as outlined below;

The discount noted in this section is specific to the agreed upon Dedicated Trucks in support of the e-Commerce Samsung Business.

- 0% Dedicated Truck discount when overdue payments are above \$6.0M
- 4% Dedicated Truck discount to start when overdue payments are below \$5.99M
- 8% Dedicated Truck discount to start when overdue payments are below \$4.0M
- 12% Dedicated Truck discount to start when overdue payments are below \$2.0M

3. As of the Effective Date, the following shall be deemed added to and made a part Schedule A of the Agreement as a new subsection 19:

Non-Standard Surface Installation Wavier: (Tile, Brick, Granite, Stone, Manufactured surface)

Samsung acknowledges that in order to properly install Samsung's Microwave Ovens or Over the Range Hoods, the Servicing Motor Carriers may be required to drill into non-standard surfaces. If during installation a non-standard surface were to crack as a result of drilling such required holes, Samsung agrees that it will not hold RXOLM or its Servicing Motor Carriers liable for the replacement of the cracked non-standard surface. Notwithstanding the foregoing, RXOLM or its Servicing Motor Carriers may be held liable for non-standard surface replacement if Samsung reasonably determines that the Servicing Motor Carriers acted negligently in the installation process.

For purposes of this letter, the following list gives examples of acts of negligence, this list is not all encompassing:

- Failure to use a reasonable degree of care to avoid damage
- Failure to use the proper tools
- Failure to use proper tools in the correct manner
- Hammering into Non-Standard Surface

If non-standard surface is cracked during the installation process, Samsung requests that RXOLM work with the onsite builder representative to determine next steps and if the crack surface needs to be replaced. Cases will exist where the builder will accept the cracked surface because it is hidden from view. In the event that the surface needs to be replaced, RXOLM will notify Samsung.

For the avoidance of doubt, this waiver only applies to non-standard surface damage as a result of drilling required to complete installation. The waiver does not apply to any other types of non-standard surface damage.
